Efficient & Compact 5-Week Female Packing List

Basically what I packed for Thailand, Taiwan, Japan, and Korea in a duffel and a day backpack.

Posted on June 24, 2019

Based on my prior experiences lugging a giant suitcase up and down subways in Asia, I wanted to make my life easier to take with me as I traversed 8 cities.

My Situation: 5-Week Trip to Eastern Asia between late September to early November. A quick Google search told me the weather in Thailand was still hot and humid, but the temperatures would get progressively colder as the trip went on. My partner and I did a lot of sightseeing around the city, so plenty of walking, but no intense nature adventures. We didn’t have fancy events to go to either (maybe when we have more money). I did purchase an extra jacket and more fall clothing in Japan and Korea because the fall weather was colder than my southern California self expected. I also have a mild shopping problem, but what I packed got me through over half of the trip.

Here's my list!

Packing Vessels:

  • Skyway Whidby Duffel

    • This duffel carried 90% of what I initially packed, and held all the souvenirs I brought back, along with some of my partner’s items. It’s got plenty of pockets, a nice thick fabric, and it comes in a cute color. I didn’t want a black duffel because it would’ve been difficult to spot in a sea of black luggage in the event I ever needed to check the bag in (which I only did on my return trip). The shoulder strap is also detachable!

  • Kipling City Pack

    • I carried my daily essentials and important items (passport, wallet, etc) around with me everywhere I went in this backpack. It has a lot of room and pockets and it’s made well!

  • Packing Cubes

    • Amazon has tons of brands. I just used what my mom had at home.
